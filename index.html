About Me

Hi, my name is Rick. I'm 30 years old and a father of 3 kids, I happen to be married to a wonderful woman and have two dogs, which makes a very full house. I am employed with miami dade county as a heavy equipment operator but also enrolled at The University of Miami in a Full-stack web development bootcamp which will provide me the tools and skills to advance my career.

Over the years I've gained many hobbies. For example, I love fishing. There's just something about being in front of a body of water and trying to catch yourself some dinner. I also enjoy a round of golf from time to time. Golf is one of the most challenging sports I have ever taken part in. After 10 years of playing I'm still pretty bad at it but there has been progress. To bring it all together I really enjoy taking my family out boating, that says it all about my hobbies.

I recently decided on a complete change in career. The reasons for the want for a change are plentiful but can be summed up to these three things:

  • Ability to better provide for my family

  • More fulfillment from my career.

  • More flexibility and options for careers.
